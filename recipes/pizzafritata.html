Pizza Fritata

Description

Looking for an easy frittata recipe? This Pizza Frittata from Delish.com is the best.



Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp. extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 onion diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • kosher salt
  • 8 large eggs
  • 2 tsp. dried oregano
  • 2 tbsp. skim milk
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • 1/2 c. shredded mozzarella
  • 1/4 c. sliced pepperoni


Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 425Â°.
  2. In a large oven-proof skillet over medium heat, heat oil.
  3. Add onions and garlic and cook until tender, 5 minutes, then add spinach. Cook until wilted, 2 to 3 minutes, and season with salt.
  4. In a large bowl, whisk together eggs, oregano, and milk and season with salt and pepper.
  5. Pour eggs over vegetables in skillet and cook until edges are cooked, 4 to 5 minutes.
  6. Top with cheese and pepperoni and transfer to the oven to bake until eggs are cooked through, 5 to 6 minutes more.
